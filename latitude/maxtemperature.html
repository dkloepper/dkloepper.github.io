Latitude vs. Max Temperature

  1. Home
  2. Max Temperature

A high maximum daily temperature has a strong relationshiop to latitudes nearer the equator (0 degrees latitude).

Given the proximity to the equinox, when the sun is most directly over the equator, it is not surprising to see a balance in temperatures between the northern and sourthern hemispheres.
